​Meet the team behind the Booksy. We’re an award-winning software startup dedicated to providing business solutions for the health and beauty industry. We developed a simple, mobile management system for service professionals.

Our company is working to bring some of the most offline industries into the digital age. Booksy was the first truly mobile scheduling platform for health and beauty businesses and has been a leader in online appointment management. With a team of 90 and growing, we are expert in software development, data-driven science and online marketing.

Booksy is the most-used app in the health and beauty industry worldwide - here's why:

We're responding to a real consumer-based demand. Despite the vast size of the industry, less than 5% of health and beauty bookings are done online – 10 times less than bookings in comparable fields (e.g. hotel and hospitality). By responding to a client need for a digital booking, we have provided businesses with dramatic benefits. With Booksy, clients make 20-30% more appointments. Of these client-based appointments, 60% are made outside of working hours.
